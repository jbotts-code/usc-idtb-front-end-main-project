About Me

Jonathon Botts is a thoughtful designer as I utilize my background in design, sustainability, business management and sociology to achieve a clean system of design making. Circular design has been a major role in my career in the creative industry, while also identifying consumerism more effectively to initiate customer bases more effectively.

My work background focuses on product design within Apparel and home decor to achieve product making at a high level. Scientific research is applied to analyze waste management concerns and tackle supply chain challenges through a sustainable framework. Other research I've developed have been for customer market research to strengthen marketing strategies by applying sociological perspectives and methods.
