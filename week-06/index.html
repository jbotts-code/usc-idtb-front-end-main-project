About Me

Jonathon Botts is a thoughtful designer as I utilize my background in design, sustainability, business management, and sociology to achieve a clean system of design making. Circular design has been a major role in my career in the creative industry, while also identifying consumerism more effectively to initiate customer bases more effectively.

My work background focuses on product design within apparel, home decor, to achieve product making at a high level. While scientific research is applied to analyze waste management concerns and tackle supply chain challenges through a sustainable framework. Other research I've developed has been for customer market research to strengthen marketing strategies by applying sociological perspectives and methods.

I carry a bachelor's degrees in sustainability, sociology, and a minor in natural resources from Oregon State University. The purpose of my education is to better understand the relationship between climate science, human behavioral science and design, because they share a similar foundation. I also am currently pursuing a master's degree at the University of Southern California in integrated design, tech and business that covers physical design, business and computer engineering. This degree focuses on construction that teaches skills to create functional products, and highlights marketing and branding tools to prepare me to shape my own entrepreneurial goals and apply machine learning towards web and app development for further design communication.
